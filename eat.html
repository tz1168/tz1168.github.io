09.22 - 09.29 First Week

This week I created this page on my own to update my progress throughout the semester and as an addition to my portfolio website. I looked through all the information on the Ability Project Website and started thinking of ways to improve content as well as the accessility of the site. I also read and looked at the videos on the Digital Accessibility website.

09.29 - 10.06 Second Week

This week I watched two videos on the Accessibility NYC YouTube Channel:

  • 1. Designing for Assistive Tech in Adobe XD- Cameron Cundiff
  • 2. Designing With Accessibility in Mind- Regine Gilbert

and also a video from Sparbox called Understanding Accessibility: WCAG’s 13 Guidelines with Kasey Bonifacio which is a brief introduction to WCAG 2.1. The presenter talked about the overall structure of WCAG and brokedown the four principles (perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust) within the 13 guidelines. I think this is good starting point for me to look more into WCAG in the following weeks.
