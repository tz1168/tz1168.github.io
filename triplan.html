Project Intro

Trips and vacations are always exciting and exhilarating. However, not everyone enjoys the preproduction process of making plans and searching all over the internet to get to the right places. In order to make a perfect travel plan, one has to go through different kinds of app such as, Google for famous tour sites, Yelp for restaurants, Google maps for directions, Calendar for recording and scheduling, and many more...

What you need is a all-in-one app that can do it all! A travel planning app that works as a travel calendar and also a search engine for publish crowd-sourced reviews about businesses and scenic spots. No need to swtich to other apps for navigation or reviews. Stick to us and we'll do it all!

The Problem

Problem Statement

People like travelers need a way to plan and organize trips in a more convenient way, which can be resolved by a travel planning app that works as a travel calendar and and also a search engine for publish crowd-sourced reviews.

The Research

Interviews

I conducted face to face interviews with my classmates and family members to see what different people need when traveling.

For the people who enjoy planning out everything before going on trips, what frustrates them is that when things could go accordingly to the plans (e.g. the museum is closed for the holiday, a beautiful place is under construction). Since these people collected information from all over the place, they often times couldn't keep up with everything everywhere. They need a solution that can organize the information they got and keep them updated.

Many others don't enjoy the process of planning before going on trips. In fact, a lot of them just book the flight tickets and the hotel, and totally go with the flow while traveling. A few people did mention that they weren't aware of many cool places because they did not take the time to search for it, and regret not going to somewhere after returning from the trip.

Expectations for the app
  • Responsive on desktop and mobile (plan on desktop and see on mobile)
  • Pre-planned trips to customize for different kinds of travellers(business,family, friends, solo)
  • Neccesary translation tools for the destination
  • Easy to navigation
